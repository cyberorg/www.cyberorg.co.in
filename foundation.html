Get started by familiarizing with Linux. Linux operating knowledge is must have qualification for being relevant in todays every changing IT scenarios.

Here is the outline of the 1 day training workshop to get you started on a journey of exploration

  • Installing Linux and installation basics
  • Using the Linux Desktop
  • Locating and Using Help Resources
  • Configuring the Network using NetworkManager
  • Managing Software
  • Managing Virtualization with Oracle VirtualBox

The workshop will be 1 full day. Participation certificate, presentation and other course material will be provided.

For whom?

This course is for students and IT administrators who want a taste of Linux, to explore if Linux system administration as a career is a right fit. It is for anyone who want extra certificate on their resume without committing too much time and money. And for someone who just needs a pointers to accelerated self-learning.

What cost?

Participation fee will be Rs 5000/-(Rs Five Thousand only) per participant for a limited number of prticipants.
