About Us


Our company is a small dream to do something big. Our core expertise is in providing Linux Solutions and training.

We are also into software development, specializing in Linux, Java and Networking.

We have also developed a hardware peripheral device- A infrared Remote Control for PC. We are perhaps the only Indian company to have developed such a device.

The company is a partnership firm under the leadership of two very capable individuals.

Mr Virat Gohil is the Chairman of the company. He is currently pursuing MCA at Nirma University, Ahmedabad. He is into his final semester project with Cyberwerx (www.cyberwerx.com).

Mr Jigish Gohil, Managing Director, has years of experience in IT field, having implemented Linux solutions sucessfully at various levels. He also has a good experience in corporate training.

We also have a technical backing of senior Linux Guru Mr Nilesh Vaghela of ElectroMech. ElectroMech is a leading Linux training and solutions provider in Gujarat and also are training and channel partner of Redhat.


 We have the know-how and expertise in handling Linux infrastructure at all levels.
Being a Novell partner enables us to offer Novell and Suse products and solutions with confidence.
We are a young and enthusiastic team, passionate about technology.

When you come to us for Linux solutions, be assured we do our best to understand your needs and offer solutions which best meet your expectations. We treat you as a partner, not merely as a customer.
